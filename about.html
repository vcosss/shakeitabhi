Abhishek Yadavㅤ

Hi, my name is Abhishek Yadav. I am an artist, designer, and content creator (YouTuber). I specialize in experience design, having completed a degree in Human Centered Design from the Srishti Institute of Art, Design and Technology in Bengaluru, India. I am confident, bold, and creative, and I love making interesting videos in public.

I have multiple hobbies, including vlogging, sketching, singing, writing songs and poems, playing cricket, traveling, videography, and video editing. I run three YouTube channels:

1. ShakeITaBhi: My main vlog channel where I capture important and interesting moments of my life, creating memories that entertain and inspire. Initially focused on my college life, the content includes insights about art and design colleges, college fests, and more. Now that I've graduated in 2024, I aim to explore and capture the world beyond college. This channel is a mix of knowledge and entertainment, and I post here most regularly.

2. The Star Lion: This channel showcases my performances, including singing, poetry, rapping, dancing, and stand-up comedy. I write my own songs, poems, rap songs, and comedy scripts. "The Star Lion" is my stage name. Although this was my main channel initially, I became more consistent on ShakeITaBhi. I upload on The Star Lion only when I perform.

3. The Real Abhishek Yadav: A motivational and inspirational channel where I share my personal thoughts, inspire people, and offer hope and motivation. This was my first face-recording channel, and it helped me gain confidence in speaking on camera.

Before these three active channels, I started with a gaming channel called ShinyEagleGaming during the COVID-19 pandemic. This was my first step on YouTube, where I recorded and uploaded gameplay videos. Although it didn't gain much traction, it gave me the confidence to start a new channel with face recordings, leading to the creation of The Real Abhishek Yadav.

My journey on YouTube has taught me many things and helped me grow as a person. Now, my goal is to create an ecosystem with all my channels, offering a space for knowledge, fun, and entertainment. I invite you to explore everything from my YouTube videos to my design work on this website. This platform connects you to all my content and services. In the future, I aim to keep upgrading both myself and this website.

Join me on this interesting journey to become better versions of ourselves and inspire others, all while having fun and being entertained. Let’s shake up minds together.
